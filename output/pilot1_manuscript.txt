TITLE: Autonomous Systematic Review: SGLT2 inhibitor vs placebo for cardiovascular death or heart failure hospitalization in heart failure

ABSTRACT
----------------------------------------
Background: This autonomous systematic review evaluated the effect of SGLT2 inhibitor compared with placebo on cardiovascular death or heart failure hospitalization in heart failure.
Methods: ClinicalTrials.gov was searched systematically. 83 records were identified, 83 screened, and 2 RCTs included in DerSimonian-Laird random-effects meta-analysis.
Results: The pooled HR 0.74 (95% CI 0.68 to 0.82), p=0.0000, was statistically significant favoring SGLT2 inhibitor. Heterogeneity was low (I2=0%, tau2=0.0000). GRADE certainty: Low.
Conclusion: Limited evidence supports SGLT2 inhibitor for cardiovascular death or heart failure hospitalization in heart failure. All trials industry-sponsored (potential bias); Limited direct evidence (k<3); Too few studies to assess publication bias.
